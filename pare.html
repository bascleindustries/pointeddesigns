The gestures

Four moves. That's the whole app.

Each item appears on its own, like a print on a gallery wall. You decide, it settles, the next one arrives.

Pare

Swipe left to let it go.

Keep

Swipe right and it stays, exactly where it was.

Defer

Swipe up if you can't decide. You'll come back to it.

Organize

Long-press a kept item to place it in an album, group, or folder.

The surfaces

Six places your phone quietly fills up.

Pare works through each one the same way — a few minutes at a time, whenever you have them.

Photos

Duplicates, blurry shots, and seven takes of the same sunset.

Screenshots

Recipes, receipts, and conversations — read and sorted for you.

Contacts

Duplicates, no-name numbers, and people from three phones ago.

Calendar

Old events, declined invitations, recurring things that ended.

Reminders

Completed lists and errands from another season of your life.

Files

Large files and old downloads you never meant to keep.
